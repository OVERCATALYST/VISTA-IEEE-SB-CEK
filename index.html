About VISTA

The VISTA is a two day Industrial Summit by IEEE SB CEK in association with IEEE IAS SBC CEK, bringing together innovators, investors, and industries on one powerful platform. Designed to transform breakthrough ideas into real-world products, the event offers idea pitching opportunities, expos featuring robotics, drones, and emerging technologies.
